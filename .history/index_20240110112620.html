Boston PD Overtime Analysis

The Boston Police Overtime Budget has been under criticism in recent years due to its excessive overspending and lack of transparency. Police Details take up a significant portion of the overtime budget. Using data acquired through FOIA requests and provided by the Boston City Council, this project analyzes how details are being paid for, where they are happening, and who is working on them.
